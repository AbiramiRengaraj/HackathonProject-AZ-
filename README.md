# HackathonProject-AZ-
Cervical Screening Engagement Platform

# Description:
This project aims to increase participation in cervical cancer screening by engaging women earlier through personalized communication and educational tools.

# Problem Statement
Many women do not attend their first cervical screening appointment. 
Missing the first screening often leads to lower participation in future screenings.

# Solution
This platform engages women 2–3 years before their first screening invitation by providing awareness, reminders, and educational content.

## Features
- Early awareness platform
- Personalized reminders
- Educational resources
- Engagement through university communities

# Hackathon
This was developed during the HealthXher Hackathon sponsored by AstraZeneca
